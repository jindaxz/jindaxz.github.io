Jinda Zhang, Aanchan Mohan
Interspeech 25

Enhancing AAC Software for Dysarthric Speakers in e-Health Settings: An Evaluation Using TORGO
Macarious Hui, Jinda Zhang, Aanchan Mohan
IEEE ICC 25
Paper
